What are in-app purchases on Natively?

In-app purchases allow users to buy digital content directly within the app, enhancing user experience and revenue generation.

How does Natively support background geolocation?

Background geolocation tracks user location even when the app is not actively in use, ideal for navigation and delivery apps.

Can I send push notifications through Natively?

Yes, Natively supports push notifications to engage users with timely updates and reminders.

Does Natively integrate with Health Kit?

Yes, Health Kit integration allows apps to access health data for fitness and medical applications.

Is biometric authorization available?

Biometric authorization, including fingerprint and facial recognition, is supported for enhanced security.

How can I use the camera functionality?

Natively provides access to the device’s camera for features like QR scanning, photo capture, and video recording.

What are toast and banner notifications?

Toast and banner notifications are subtle alerts that appear on the screen to provide important information without disrupting the user experience.
